Important: What's Not Included in Our Pricing

Please read before placing an order — these are separate third-party costs paid directly to providers

Web Hosting

Server space to keep your website live is billed directly by the hosting provider (e.g. Hostinger, GoDaddy, Bluehost). This is not included in our service pricing.

Domain Name

Your website address (e.g. yourname.com) is purchased separately from a domain registrar. Annual renewal charges apply and are not covered in our quotes.

Deployment & Infrastructure

Third-party deployment platforms, SSL certificates, CDN services, or cloud infrastructure (AWS, Vercel, Netlify, etc.) are billed independently by those providers.

Our pricing covers 100% of design & development work only. We'll guide you step-by-step through setting up hosting and a domain — and can assist with the process — but those costs go directly to the provider, not to us. All project quotes are discussed transparently before any work begins.
